Road Map

Liquidity Pool:

A portion of the transaction fees is allocated to the liquidity pool, enhancing liquidity and stabilizing the coin's value within the market.

Burn Mechanism

A deflationary mechanism is implemented by periodically burning a portion of the LMAO Coins, reducing the overall supply and potentially increasing the value of each remaining coin.

Community Governance

LMAO Coin holders have a say in the coin's development and proposals through a decentralized governance model. This ensures a democratic decision-making process within the community.

Partnerships and Collaborations

Collaborations with other projects, influencers, or charitable organizations may be integrated into the tokenomics to create additional value and promote community growth.

Total Supply

The total supply of LMAO Coins is capped to create scarcity and drive value. Initially set at a predetermined amount, this ensures the coin remains a prized asset.

Initial Distribution

LA portion of LMAO Coins is distributed during the token's launch to early adopters, supporters, and community members. This promotes widespread ownership and engagement from the outset.

Rewards and Incentives

Holders of LMAO Coins are incentivized through regular rewards in the form of additional coins. This encourages long-term holding, fostering a loyal and engaged community.

Transaction Fees

A nominal transaction fee is charged for each buy and sell transaction. A portion of these fees is redistributed to existing LMAO Coin holders, promoting continuous participation and community growth.
